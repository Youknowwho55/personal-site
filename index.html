Who is Dylan?

Bio written by AI

Hello there! Welcome to my personal website! I'm Dylan, and I'm all about learning new hobbies and passions. I truly believe in the famous quote form James Clear's Atomic Habits "If you can get 1% better each day for one year, you’ll end up 37 times better by the time you’re done." (-James Clear, Atomic Habits: An Easy and Proven Way to Build Good Habits and Break Bad Ones).

I've spent the last 6+ years diving deep into the Finance and Mortgage industry, gaining valuable experience and knowledge that have continue to shape my day to day life. Along the way, I've had the opportunity to connect with truly extraordinary individuals, whom I've made it my goal to make a positive difference for each other.

Now it's time to explore additional fields of interest away from the Financial realm, like what you see here in this website!

While here, you'll find a collection of my work, including, but not limited to, recent passion projects, useful coding projects, 3D designs/CAD projects and hopefully more to come. I take great pride in striving to make each day one that is both productive and fulfilling and I'd love to have you along the ride towards achieving these results!

Through this website and additional Blog and Podcast posts my aim is to provide valuable resources that empower eveyone to get creative and try a hobby they have always dreamed of.

Feel free to explore the website and don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions, collaboration ideas, or potential opportunities. I'm always ready to connect!

Thank you a ton for dropping by, and I can't wait to start this incredible journey together!

Stay tuned,

Dylan Rayburn
